WiFi and Check-In / Check-Out Times

These are by far are three most common questions!  1) What is the WiFi password?  2) Can we check in early?  3) Can we check out late?

We get it, we ask the same questions when we stay somewhere.  The answers are:

1) We will send you the WiFi password via email before your stay, we have the WiFi password in our guest book which will be available when you enter the property, and we have a free standing picture frame with the WiFi password in the common area.  (We STILL get asked - but don't worry, we're happy to answer this question :)  )

2) Checking in Early : We allow this if two things occur a) we don't have departing guests the day you arrive AND b) our cleaners confirm that they can finish before check-in time.  We generally do not and cannot know if we will have departing guests until very close to your arrival date as we get last minute bookings all the time.  The best practice is to check with us the night before you arrive.

3) Checking out Late : We allow this if three things occur a) we don't have arriving guests the day you depart AND b) our cleaners confirm that they can start cleaning later than the check-out time AND c) your party is willing to strip the pillow sheets/bed-linens and start a load of laundry with them prior to departing. We generally do not and cannot know if we will have arriving guests until very close to your departure date as we get last minute bookings all the time. The best practice is to check with us the night before you depart.
